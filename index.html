    ABOUT HACK-A-THON

    Hackathon 2k22, a National Level Hackathon, is going to be organized by College of Engineering Roorkee, Roorkee(Uttarakhand), India on 18 - 19 November. Hackathon 2k22 is an event that brings together Developers, Hackers and Technology enthusiasts to develop innovative solutions to the challenges in society, industry and the environment. It's a fantastic opportunity for developing indigenous tech solutions that are innovative and can be turned into a functional prototype with the help of industry mentors.

    Event Details

    Round 1

    18th November, 2022

    The first round of the event will be held on November 18, 2022 at College of Engineering Roorkee campus. The judges will award points based on the progress made before the first round.

    Round 2

    19th November, 2022

    The second round of the event will be held on November 19, 2022 at College of Engineering Roorkee campus. The judges will award points to the teams based on the progress made before the second round and the preparation of the PPT on their project.

    Round 3

    19th November, 2022

    The third and final round of the event will be held on November 19, 2022 at College of Engineering Roorkee campus. Judges will award teams points based on their final product developed during the 36 hours of the event and the PPT they create on their project.

    Themes

    Infrastructure

    Infrastructure being the underlying structure of a country and its economy has a huge potential of scaling it. Dive into cracking various infrastructure problems such as regulatory risks, environmental impact, land acquisition and many more.

    Travel and Tourism

    With the world opening up there is a need to travel uphills for making a mark in travel and tourism. Decode travel and tourism in a way that it boosts efficiency and effectiveness for the travel and tourism industry by having a crack at problems such as access and connectivity, sustainable and sufficient accommodation and proper amenities, tourists security and many more.

    Smart Education

    With the world drifting towards technology, why not make room for smart education. So indulge yourself in tracking innovative solutions for problems such as Privacy and security of students, online work tracking, virtual class engagement and many more.

    Smart Agriculture

    Farming is all about getting in touch with earth around us so, enhancing it with technology, why not? Go ahead and thrive for innovative solutions for Farming with the art and science that manages precise cultivation, soil monitoring, crop sowing, etc.

    Health Care

    The art of medicine consists of amusing the patients while nature cures the disease. Go ahead and innovate health care system problems related to patients data management, healthcare cost, evidence based emergency treatments and many more.

    FAQs

    What is the team size for the Hackathon 2k22?

    Team can consist of at least 3 team members to a maximum of 5 members including 1 Team Leader. Each team must have at least one female team member.

    What is the registration fees?

    Only the shortlisted team will have to pay the registration amount of ₹200 per team.

    Can working professionals participate in the Hackathon 2k22?

    No, students who are pursuing any UG or PG courses can participate in Hackathon 2k22.

    Do we need to provide any proof to participate in Hackathon 2k22?

    Yes, the teams who will be shortlisted must present a letter of consent from their college/university, along with a copy of their student ID as verification.

    Can multiple ideas be registered for one team?

    No.

    Can we change team members after round 1?

    No, you cannot change your team once registered.

    How do I know if my idea has been selected or shortlisted?

    Shortlisted team will be notified through mail or phone call.

    Will travel allowance and accommodation be provided?

    Only accommodation will be provided by us. The teams will have to manage the travel expenses themselves.
